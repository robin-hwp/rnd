01 관계대명사 what
* what = 선행사를 포함한 관계 대명사
* 역할 : 선행사 + 접속사 + 대명사
- what = 선행사 + { 관계 대명사 (= 접속사 + 대명사 ) }
- what절은 명사절에 해당하고 명사절은 주어, 목적어, 보어의 역할.

Schubert just produced music. And music was in him, and he brought us a rich treasure of music.
-> Schubert just produced music which(= And music) was in him, and he brought us a rich treasure of music.
-> Schubert just produced what was in him, and he brought us a rich treasure of music.

Faith is believing the thing. And we cannot prove it.
-> Faith is believing the thing which we cannot prove.
-> Faith is believing what we cannot prove.
* 믿음은 우리가 증명할 수 엇는 것을 믿는 것이다.

I remember the people who(m) we met in Paris.
-> 선행사가 사람인 경우 what으로 변경할 수 없다.
Language is a tool which links us to people around the world.
-> Language is what links us to people around the world.

What(= The thing which) I want to do in the future is to teach students English.
Reading novels is what(= the thing which) I enjoy most.

02 전치사 + 관계 대명사
* 선행사에 따라서 관계 부사가 될 수 있느냐가 결정 된다.

This is thy gym. I like to work out at the gym.
-> This is the gym which I like to work out at.
-> This is the gym where I like to work out.

I am working for the store. I bought a T-shirt in this store.
-> I am working for the store which I bought T-shirt in.
-> I am working for the store where I bought T-shirt.

* 전치사 + that을 사용할 수 없다.
This is the gym that I like to work out at. ( O )
This is the gym at that I like to work out. ( X )

03 관계 대명사 앞 콤마(,)
1) 계속적 용법 ( 콤마 사용 : 접속사 + 대명사로 해석 )
2) 한정적 용법 ( 콤마 없음 : 관계 대명사절이 선행사를 수식하도록 해석 )

I dropped the cup, which knocked over the eggs, whitch went all over the kitchen floor.
-> 나는 컵을 떨어뜨렸고, 그 컵은 달걀 위에 떨어졌고, 그 달걀들이 깨져서 부엌 바닥이 난장판이 되었다.
I like the song which you are singing now.
-> 내가 지금 부르고 있는 그 노래를 나는 줗아한다.
* 관계 대명사 that 앞에는 콤마(,)를 사용할 수 없다.

03 생략이 가능한 관계사
1) 목적격 관계 대명사
    - 목적격 관계 대명사는 문장에서 생략 가능 
2) 주격 관계 대명사 + be 동사
    - 주격 관계 대명사는 뒤에 be 동사와 함께 쓰일 때, 둘이 함께 생략 가능 ( 둘정에 하나만 생략은 불가 )
3) 관계 부사의 생략
    - 관계 부사를 사용할 때 선행사나 관계 부사 중 하나를 생략하여 쓸수도 있다.

The man (whom) I marry will have a good sense of humor.
I am watching the man (who is) wearing a funny cap.
I don't know the reason why my license was canceled.
I don't know the reason my license was canceled.
I don't know why my license was canceled.
Tomorrow is Saturday when we will go swimming.
Tomorrow is Saturday we will go swimming.
Tomorrow is when we will go swimming.
This is the room where I met you.
This is the room I met you.
This is where I met you.

예외 - the way와 how는 같이 쓸 수 없다. 둘중에 하나만 가능
Internet is changing the way. People shop in this way.
Internet is changing the way in which people shop.
Internet is changing how people shop. ( O )
Internet is changing the way people shop. ( O )
Internet is changing the way how people shop. ( X )
