1. 절
1) 절이란? - 주어와 서술어가 있는 단어 덩이
2) 절의 종류와 역할
    - 주절
    - 종속절 ( 언제나 접속사 뒤에 온다. 보통 절이 2개 보이면 접속사 뒤에 보이는 절이 종속절이라고 보면 된다. )
    (1) 명사절 ( 문장속에서 주어/목적어/보어 역할 )
    (2) 부사절 ( 명사, 형용사 역할을 제외한 나머지 역할. 이유, 시간, 조건, 목적 등의 의미를 가짐 )
    (3) 형용사절 ( 명사를 꾸며주는 역할 )

    - Whether you like her or not is not an issue here.
    - I know that you love me.
    - My phone is that he will come back soon.
    - Because I woke up early this morning, I feel sleepy.
    - I've decided to be a carpenter when I grow up.
    - If you miss it, you will regret it.
    - THis is a blouse which I bought last week.
    - This is a room where I met him.

    - I strongly recommend that you read this book.
    - Seoul is a city which I want to visit.
    - If it rains tomorrow, we will stay at home.


2. 접속사
1) 접속사란? - 단어와 단어, 구와 구, 절과 절을 연결시켜주는 단어
2) 접속사의 종류
    (1) 등위 접속사 ( 단어와 단어, 구와 구, 절과 절 )
    - and, but, or, so

    - Pizzas and pastas are on the menu.
    - You may send the resume by mail or by fax.
    - I am sorry, but I love you.
    - He had homework, so he stayed up late to finish it.
    - She was in the classroom, and he went there to see her.

    (2) 상관 접속사 - 서로 상관하는 것들을 연결시켜 주는 접속사 ( 숙어의 형태 )
    - both A and B(A와 B 둘 다), either A or B(A 또는 B), neither A nor B(A도 아니고 B도 아닌),
      not only A but also B (A뿐만아니라 B도), B as well as A (A뿐만 아니라 B도), not A but B(A가 아니라 B)
    - both A and B는 복수 동사를 사용하고, 나머지는 핵주어 B에 맞춰 동사를 사용한다.

    - Both Pizza and Pastas are on the menu.
    - You may send the resume either by fax or by mail.
    - Neither he nor you are responsible for this situation.
    - I did not only my homework, but I also cleaned the room.
    - He as well as I love you.
    - Life is not about speed but about direction.
      
    (3) 종속 접속사 - 대등하지 않은 절과 절을 연결
    - whether(if), that, wh-로 시작하는 의문사 ( when, what, where, who, why)
      because, although, if, while, before, after, even if
    
    - I don't know if he will come.

* 종속절의 종류
- It is certain that he is innocent.
- The weather forecast said that there would be rain today.
- I wonder if/whether you like this sweater.
- Who said it is important to me.
- I can't understand what he said.
- I have the belief that you will achieve your dream someday.
- 'Superman returns' is a movie which I like the most.
- Seoul is a city where I spent my childhood.
- When he came home, I turned on the TV to watch the show.
- I missed the bus because I got up late in the morning.
- Although it was freezing outside, it was warm inside thanks to the heating system.
- I will wait for you if you promise to come.
- While I was having breakfast, my sister overslept herself and skipped breakfast.
